## 5-Stage Agency Workflow
### Stage 1: Intake & Research
- **Goal:** Analyze client idea & market feasibility.
- **Process:** Perplexity AI analyzes the input for market trends and competitors.
- **Output:** Feasibility Report & Market Analysis.

### Stage 2: Agency Architecture
- **Goal:** Design the system architecture.
- **Process:** Specialist agents design the database schema and component structure.
- **Output:** Architecture Diagram & Tech Stack Selection.

### Stage 3: Construction
- **Goal:** Execute the build.
- **Process:** Automated coding via Antigravity agents (Backend/Frontend).
- **Output:** Functional Codebase & Database Schema.

### Stage 4: Optimization
- **Goal:** Polish and secure the application.
- **Process:** Lighthouse performance auditing & Security hardening.
- **Output:** Optimization Report & Security Audit.

### Stage 5: Handoff
- **Goal:** Deliver the product to the client.
- **Process:** Deployment to Vercel & Client Dashboard access.
- **Output:** Live URL & Admin Credentials.
